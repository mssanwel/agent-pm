# Agent PM — Learning Log

Rolling log of human corrections and confirmations. Read during triage (last ~20 entries) and consolidated weekly. Entries older than 30 days are moved to `learning-log-archive.md` by the weekly trigger.

## Entry format

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD

### MSS-N — <skill>
- **Outcome**: approved | corrected | rejected
- **Correction**: <what the human changed/said — paste the comment verbatim when short>
- **Lesson**: <generalisable rule — the thing future runs should apply>
```

Keep lessons generalisable. A good lesson reads like a registry change waiting to happen: "when the issue mentions X, always do Y" / "never post a draft email without approval".
